
# AngelBox Professional Referral Eligibility Checker

A structured eligibility assessment tool designed for **professionals and organisations** to determine whether a referral may meet AngelBox criteria.

This tool supports consistent, efficient, and fair triage of referrals prior to submission.

---

## 📌 Overview

The AngelBox Professional Referral Eligibility Checker is designed for:

- Midwives  
- Social workers  
- Health professionals  
- Charitable organisations  
- Support services  

It enables referrers to quickly confirm whether a case is likely to meet eligibility requirements before proceeding with a full referral.

---

## ✅ Key Features

- ✅ Full London borough coverage  
- ✅ Service area eligibility filtering  
- ✅ Residency status classification  
- ✅ Household income threshold checks  
- ✅ Company director exclusion logic  
- ✅ Financial context via benefits capture  
- ✅ Due date timing validation  
- ✅ Evidence requirement verification  
- ✅ Clear professional decision outcomes  
- ✅ Mobile-friendly, responsive design  

---

## 🎯 Eligibility Criteria (Summary)

Referrals are assessed based on:

- Borough of residence (must be within supported areas)  
- Residency status  
- Household income threshold  
- Company director status  
- Financial context (benefits or equivalent)  
- Pregnancy timing (within service window)  
- Ability to provide supporting evidence  

---

## 🧭 How It Works

The tool guides referrers through a short sequence of structured checks:

1. Borough verification  
2. Residency status  
3. Employment context  
4. Income threshold  
5. Company director status  
6. Benefits / financial support  
7. Due date or birth timing  
8. Evidence availability  

At the end of the process, the tool provides:

- ✅ Confirmation of likely eligibility  
- ❌ Clear explanation where criteria are not met  

---

## ✅ Outcome

If criteria are met, the tool will confirm:

**"This referral appears to meet initial eligibility criteria"**

Referrers should then proceed to submit a full application.

---

## ⚠️ Important

- This tool provides **initial eligibility guidance only**  
- Submission of a referral **does not guarantee support**  
- All cases are subject to review and verification by the AngelBox team
